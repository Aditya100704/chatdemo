Renting a Flat or House in India Without a Broker — How RenterFinder Properties Work

Finding a flat for rent in India has traditionally meant either waiting for a broker to share options from their limited personal network or scrolling through large portals that mix rental and sale listings, making it difficult to identify what is actually available for rent right now. RenterFinder.com was built to fix this. Every property listed here is posted directly by a landlord — no broker intermediary, no commission markup, and no conflict of interest in the match. The price you see is the price the landlord is willing to accept.

Rental properties on RenterFinder.com span every configuration — Studio and 1 RK apartments ideal for single professionals, 1BHK and 2BHK flats suited to couples or small families, 3BHK and larger units for bigger households, independent houses, builder floors, duplexes, and furnished apartments near IT parks, educational institutions, and business districts. Listings are available across all major metro cities — Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata — as well as tier-2 and tier-3 cities including Jaipur, Lucknow, Patna, Bhopal, Ranchi, Nagpur, Surat, Indore, Coimbatore, Visakhapatnam, and hundreds more across India.

Because RenterFinder.com is a rental-exclusive platform, landlords listing here are actively seeking tenants — not exploring sale options, not testing the market, not listing speculatively. Every landlord on the platform has paid a Profile Listing Fee of ₹125 for 3 months, which filters out inactive and casual listings and ensures only serious landlords appear in search results. Renters similarly pay the same ₹125 listing fee to upload their own profile — this mutual commitment keeps the platform genuinely two-sided and free of spam.

When a renter finds a property that matches their requirements, RenterFinder.com facilitates a moderated introduction — protecting both parties' privacy until there is mutual interest. The Platform Service Fee of 12 days' rent is split into two stages: 6 days' rent to arrange the property meeting, and 6 days' rent at deal closure when the rent agreement is signed. This structure means you only pay the second half when the deal is actually finalised — aligning the platform's interest directly with yours. There are no hidden charges, no registration fees, and no escalation costs on either side.

If you are a landlord looking to find a tenant rather than wait for enquiries to arrive, RenterFinder.com also offers the Prospective Renters' List → — India's first searchable directory of renters actively looking for properties, where landlords can browse and reach out to pre-verified renters directly. Both services operate on the same transparent fee structure with zero broker commission on either side.
