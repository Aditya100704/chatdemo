Privacy Policy — RenterFinder.com

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data on India's only rental-exclusive property platform · Effective: June 07, 2025 · Last Updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") governs the collection, use, storage, protection, and disclosure of personal data by RenterFinder.com ("Platform," "Portal," "Website," "we," "us," or "our"), an online technology-based intermediary facilitating connections between landlords and renters in India. By accessing or using the Services, including www.renterfinder.com, its mobile applications, and related functionalities (collectively, the "Services"), you consent to the data practices described herein. If you do not agree, you must immediately cease use of the Services.

1. Data Collection

1.1 Categories of Data Collected:
• Personal Data: Includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, address, Aadhaar number (where legally permitted), PAN, property photos, and other identifiers provided during account registration, property listings, or interactions with the Services.
• Sensitive Personal Data: Includes payment information (e.g., bank account or credit card details), passwords, and other data classified as sensitive under the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 ("SPDI Rules").
• Non-Personal Data: Includes anonymized usage statistics, device information (e.g., IP address, browser type, operating system), location data, cookies, web beacons, and log data.

1.2 Methods of Collection: Data is collected:
• Directly from you when you register, create listings, post user-generated content (e.g., reviews), or communicate with us or other Users.
• Automatically through cookies, web beacons, server logs, or other tracking technologies when you interact with the Services.
• From third parties, such as payment processors or analytics providers, where necessary to facilitate or enhance the Services.

1.3 Mobile-Specific Data: For mobile application users, we may request access to:
• Location Data: To provide geographically relevant property listings.
• Phone Numbers: For authentication and service-related communications.
• Phonebook Contacts: To facilitate referrals, with your explicit consent.
• Installed Application Data: To detect and prevent fraud, ensuring secure transactions.
You may deny or revoke these permissions via device settings, though this may limit certain Service functionalities.

1.4 Purpose of Collection: We collect data to:
• Facilitate connections between landlords and renters.
• Process transactions, verify identities, and authenticate Users.
• Enhance, personalize, and maintain the Services.
• Conduct analytics, troubleshoot issues, and improve Platform functionality.
• Deliver targeted advertisements, subject to your preferences.
• Comply with legal obligations under Indian laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000.

1.5 Accuracy of User Information: You are responsible for ensuring the accuracy and completeness of all personal data provided to us. We rely on the information you submit and are not liable for any errors, omissions, or consequences arising from inaccurate or outdated data. You may update your information via account settings or by contacting us at report@renterfinder.com.
