How RenterFinder.com is Different — Search a Renter or Tenant Directly, No Broker India

Traditional property brokers in India charge between 15 and 90 days' rent as brokerage commission — an amount that can easily run into ₹20,000 to ₹90,000 for a single rental transaction in a metro city. In most cases, the broker simply introduces you to one or two options they hold in their personal network, with little transparency on why those properties or renters were shortlisted. RenterFinder.com replaces this model entirely. Our two services — Renter Search and Property Search — operate on a self-serve, transparent platform where landlords and renters do the searching themselves, with access to a far wider pool of matches than any individual broker could offer.

Both landlords and renters pay the same two fees separately: a Profile Listing Fee of ₹125 (discounted from ₹499 — 75% off) for 3 months, and a Platform Service Fee of only 12 days' rent — split into 6 days to arrange the property meeting, and 6 days at deal closure when the agreement is signed. No upfront retainer, no hidden charges. Both sides get access to a pan-India platform with real profiles and direct contact details. No broker, no commission escalation, and no conflict of interest.
