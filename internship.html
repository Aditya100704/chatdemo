Why Intern at RenterFinder.com?

RenterFinder.com is India's first rental-only property platform — a category-defining startup at an early and exciting stage. Interns work directly with the founding team and get hands-on exposure to real challenges across content, marketing, and business development. Unlike large corporate internships where you shadow a team, here you own deliverables from day one. Your work — whether a city-level content campaign or an outreach initiative — directly impacts the platform's growth.

The internship is entirely online, making it accessible from anywhere in India. We accept applications throughout the year with no fixed intake cycle. If you are a motivated student or recent graduate with a genuine interest in proptech, rentals, content writing, digital marketing, or early-stage startups, this is an opportunity to build a portfolio of real work and earn a certificate of completion. To apply, send your CV and a brief note about yourself to the email address below.
