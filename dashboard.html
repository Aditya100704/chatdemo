How Your Dashboard Works

Your RenterFinder.com dashboard is your personal control centre for managing your rental profile or property listing. Once you log in, all your information — profile details, active listings, match notifications, and account settings — are available in one place.

👤
Manage Your Profile

Update your requirements, budget, preferred area, move-in date, and contact preferences. Keep your profile current so landlords can reach you.

🏠
Your Property Listings

If you are a landlord, view and manage all your active rental property listings. Edit rent, description, availability, and amenities at any time.

🔔
Match Notifications

Get notified when a landlord or renter expresses interest in your listing or profile. Respond quickly to increase your chances of a successful match.
